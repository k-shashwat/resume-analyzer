Senior Data Analyst

Location: Remote / San Francisco, CA
Type: Full-time

About the Role
We are looking for a Senior Data Analyst to join our analytics team. You will work closely with product, engineering, and business stakeholders to drive data-informed decisions across the organization. You will own end-to-end analytics projects — from defining metrics and building data pipelines to delivering actionable insights through dashboards and presentations.

Responsibilities
- Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for product and business growth
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports using Tableau, Power BI, or Looker
- Write complex SQL queries to extract and transform data from Snowflake, Redshift, and PostgreSQL
- Develop and maintain ETL pipelines using Python, Airflow, and dbt
- Design and analyze A/B tests, including sample size calculation, statistical significance testing, and result interpretation
- Perform statistical modeling and predictive analytics using Python (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, statsmodels)
- Collaborate with data engineering to improve data quality, governance, and documentation
- Present findings and recommendations to senior leadership and non-technical stakeholders
- Mentor junior analysts on analytical best practices, SQL optimization, and experimental design
- Support self-serve analytics by creating curated datasets and Looker explores

Required Qualifications
- 5+ years of experience in data analysis, business intelligence, or data science
- Expert-level SQL skills with experience across multiple database platforms (PostgreSQL, Snowflake, Redshift)
- Strong proficiency in Python for data analysis, including pandas, NumPy, and matplotlib/seaborn
- Hands-on experience with BI tools such as Tableau, Looker, or Power BI
- Experience designing and analyzing A/B tests and experiments
- Solid understanding of statistical concepts: hypothesis testing, regression, distributions, confidence intervals
- Experience with ETL/ELT pipelines and workflow orchestration (Airflow, dbt, or Prefect)
- Strong communication skills with the ability to present technical findings to non-technical audiences
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, or a related quantitative field

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift)
- Familiarity with Git, CI/CD, and software engineering best practices
- Experience with machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, XGBoost, LightGBM)
- Knowledge of data modeling, star schemas, and dimensional modeling
- Experience with Amplitude, Mixpanel, or Google Analytics for product analytics
- Master's degree in a quantitative field

Tech Stack You'll Work With
- Languages: Python, SQL
- Databases: PostgreSQL, Snowflake, Redshift
- BI: Tableau, Looker, Power BI
- Orchestration: Airflow, dbt
- Cloud: AWS (S3, Redshift, EC2)
- Analytics: Amplitude, Mixpanel
- Version Control: Git, GitHub
